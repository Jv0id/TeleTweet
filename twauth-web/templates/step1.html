Welcome to TeleTweet.

To get started to use TeleTweet, we'll need you to authorize TeleTweet to access your Twitter account. More information on what is required is available from Twitter's OAuth overview.

Here's the steps:

  1. Click Let's begin button.
  2. Then we will redirect you to x.com, for you to authorize our app.
  3. While at x.com you will authorize TeleTweet and you'll then be redirected back to us.
  4. Then you need to copy the token back to TeleTweet.
